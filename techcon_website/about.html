Our History

TechCon was born in 2010 from a small group of engineers and thinkers who wanted to spark deeper collaboration in the ever-expanding world of technology. Since then, TechCon has grown into a global gathering point for innovation, thought leadership, and disruptive ideas.

Our Mission

We believe technology is humanity’s greatest lever for change. Our mission is to empower people, spark ideas, and build a sustainable digital future. At TechCon, we create a platform where anyone—from coders to CTOs—can explore, connect, and grow.
